## About

_Green Horizons: Youth for Sustainable Communities_ was an Erasmus+ Youth Exchange that brought together young people from different European countries to explore sustainability, climate action, and active participation through non-formal education and intercultural learning.

Set in rural Spain, the project provided an opportunity to exchange ideas, collaborate with participants from diverse backgrounds, and reflect on the role young people can play in creating more sustainable communities.

## Main Topics

- Sustainability
- Climate action
- Environmental awareness
- Youth participation
- Gender equality
- Intercultural dialogue

## Learning Outcomes

During this youth exchange, I:

- Strengthened my understanding of sustainability and environmental challenges.
- Collaborated with participants from different cultural backgrounds.
- Explored non-formal education methods and participatory approaches.
- Developed skills in teamwork, communication, and intercultural cooperation.
- Reflected on how young people can contribute to positive change in their communities.

## Reflection

Participating in _Green Horizons_ allowed me to connect with young people from across Europe while exploring practical approaches to sustainability and community engagement. The experience broadened my perspective on environmental responsibility and reinforced the importance of international cooperation in addressing global challenges.
