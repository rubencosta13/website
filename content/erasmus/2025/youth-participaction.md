# Youth Participa(c)tion 🇷🇴

**Training Course (TC)**  
📍 Breaza, Romania  
📅 1–8 July 2025  
🏢 Organized by **A.C.T.O.R. – Asociatia Culturala pentru Teatru si Origami din Romania**

## About

*Youth Participa(c)tion* was an Erasmus+ Training Course that brought together youth workers and active young people from different countries to explore ways of strengthening youth participation in democratic life and society.

Through non-formal education methods, group activities, and intercultural exchange, participants shared experiences and developed tools to encourage active citizenship and meaningful participation among young people.

## Main Topics

- Youth participation
- Active citizenship
- Democratic engagement
- Facilitation methods
- Inclusion and accessibility
- Non-formal education

## Learning Outcomes

During this training course, I:

- Explored methods for promoting meaningful youth participation.
- Strengthened my facilitation and communication skills.
- Learned from participants with diverse cultural backgrounds.
- Gained practical experience with non-formal education approaches.
- Developed a deeper understanding of democratic processes and civic engagement.

## Reflection

Participating in *Youth Participa(c)tion* allowed me to exchange ideas with people from across Europe, broaden my perspective on active citizenship, and gain valuable tools that can be applied in youth work and community initiatives.
