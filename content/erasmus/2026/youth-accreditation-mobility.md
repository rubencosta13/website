# Youth Accreditation Mobility – Erasmus+ KA150-YOU 🇹🇷

**Youth Exchange (YE)**  
📍 Sakarya, Türkiye  
📅 28 March – 5 April 2026

## About

This Erasmus+ youth mobility brought together participants from seven European countries to foster active citizenship, leadership, and intercultural understanding through non-formal education and collaborative learning.

The project provided an opportunity to exchange perspectives with young people from different backgrounds while exploring topics such as digital competences, entrepreneurship, sustainability, and European values.

## Main Topics

- Active citizenship
- Leadership
- Digital competences
- Entrepreneurship
- Environmental sustainability
- Intercultural dialogue

## Learning Outcomes

During this project, I:

- Strengthened my leadership and communication skills.
- Collaborated with participants from diverse cultural backgrounds.
- Explored digital and entrepreneurial competences through non-formal learning.
- Developed a deeper understanding of European values and active citizenship.
- Enhanced my ability to work effectively in international teams.

## Reflection

Participating in this Erasmus+ mobility allowed me to broaden my international network and gain valuable experience in intercultural collaboration. The exchange reinforced the importance of youth participation and highlighted how cooperation and shared learning can contribute to more inclusive and sustainable communities.
